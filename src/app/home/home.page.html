Bridging the Reading Gap

Reading Pal is an online platform for teaching reading in an interactive, modern manner.

Reading Pal is the ideal environment for reading teachers and students.

By putting reading education on the web, teachers can constantly track their students' progress. Our platform also rewards students with badges and experience points as they work through material and practice reading.

Our e-reader is built for everyone

Reading Pal's integrated e-reader is packed with customization features, ensuring every student can comfortably read the assigned materials.

Teachers can easily create lessons

Not only can teachers create lessons from scratch, they can also copy other lessons or import specific components directly from the lesson builder.

Students are rewarded for their effort

Reading Pal's quiz environment looks clean and rewards students with badges their teachers create.

We also have a vocabulary game that gives students experience points based on how well they do.
